Coronavirus !


The first case of coronavirus was detected in China on November 19,2019 . It has caused more than 370000 deaths all over the world. The sympotms may be detected from 7 to 14 days after being infected by the virus. Each country in the world is taking preventive measures to protect their public from the novel coronavirus. More rarely, the disease can be serious and even fatal. Older people, and people with other medical conditions (such as asthma, diabetes, or heart disease), may be more vulnerable to becoming severely ill.

The symptons of novel coronavirus are :
  1. Cough
  2. fever
  3. tiredness
  4. (In severe cases) difficulty in breathing
  5. Lost of sense of smell and taste
Precautionary Measures :
1. STAY HOME AND STAY SAFE
2. Wash hand more often
Wash your hands for 20 seconds, with soap and water or alcohol-based hand rub
3. While coughing cover your mouth
Cover your nose and mouth with a disposable tissue or flexed elbow when you cough or sneeze
4. Dont touch your face
Don't touch your eyes, nose, or mouth if your hands are not clean
5. Wear a face mask
Always wear a face mask or cover your face with a cloth if u need to go out of your house
6. Keep distance between each preson
Avoid close contact (1 meter or 3 feet) with people who are unwell
7. SELF ISOLATE
Stay home and self-isolate from others in the household if you feel unwell

Now-a-days we have to take precautionary measures as an individual . If you have mild symptons so be at your home , take rest , keep yourself warm , drink plenty of liquids like hot water , gargle with salty water , use a room humidifier or take a hot shower to help ease a sore throat and cough so that you recover fast .

If fever originates or difficulty in breathing , immideatly get yourself checked by a doctor
